<h1 align="center" style="color:#a07aba;"> <u> Reflection for 1st Quarter </u> </h1>
<p> During the 1st Quarter, I learned about the different HTML Elements, such as Lists, Tables, Hyperlinks, and Forms. Using these elements I can improve the layout and designs of my websites. By using lists, I can present my information in a more structured format and allows information to digesst more easily. Tables help present my data in a clear and organized manner and make information more visually appealing. Hyperlinks allow me to link and navigate to different websites by just a few clicks, making it less of a hassle to find certain websites. Finally, forms provide a way for visitors to reach out, give feedbacks, and inquire about websites, making communication less time-consuming. In summary, combining these HTML elements make websites more organized, user-friendly, and engaging. Reinforcing the importance of structure, functionality, and interactivity in a webpage. </p><br><br>
